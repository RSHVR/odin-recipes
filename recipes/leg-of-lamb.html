Ingredients

  • 1 leg of lamb, 3-4 lbs
  • 2 oz butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 wild garlic, or a couple of garlic cloves
  • half an onion, chopped finely
  • 1 tsp dried mint, or 1 tbsp fresh
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs

Preparation

  1. Place leg of lamb in a roasting pan. Mix the other ingredients and spread over the meat.
  2. Place in an oven, preheated to 175 C (360 F) and roast for 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
  3. Let rest for around 10 minutes, then serve
